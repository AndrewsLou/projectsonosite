Proof of Stake

SONO combines both PoS and PoW.

Stage 1 - Proof of Work and Proof of Stake

Stage 1 of SONO will consist of a Proof of Work distribution method using the SkunkHash algorithm. The reward of each Proof of Work block is 1 SONO every 60 seconds and any additional transactions fees that should come from the transactions within the block.

Stage 1 will also consist of Proof of Stake.

The intent of Proof of Stake is to secure the network with a high amount of active nodes. It then rewards the users who keep coins in their wallets with up to 0.49% per 24 hours the coins has been in the wallet.

This means 180% in a year from Proof of Stake to start off.

This will ensure that a large amount of coins are produced in the first stage as the Proof of Work reward is set very low.

Stage 1 ended on the 20th of January 2018.

Stage 2 - Masternode Update

Stage 2 will be including the Masternode Update that was added to the project after a community vote on the official forums.

Masternodes will receive the following rewards:

  • Proof of Work Blocks: 25% of the reward.

  • Proof of Stake Blocks: 25% of the reward.

Stage 2 was activated on the 27th of January 2018.

Masternodes

SONO releases with masternode enabled. The cost of a masternode is 1000 SONO.
Masternodes are rewarded 25% of both PoW and PoS blocks.

Web Wallet

The SONO development team has created a web wallet that allows the user to stake along with a hand full of other helpful features to make your life easier.
We are constantly looking for feedback so if there is anything you'd like to see in the web wallet send us a message on our official discord server!
